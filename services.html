Services

At our car service center, we use only the latest technology and tools to diagnose and repair your vehicle. We also offer a wide range of genuine OEM parts and accessories to ensure that your vehicle is always running at its best.

Quick service

The Quick Service is a fast and efficient option for customers who need an oil change for their vehicle.The customer can then select the most convenient time for their appointment and submit their request. The Quick Service is designed to minimize the time spent by the customer and get them back on the road as quickly as possible.

Diagnostic service

The Service Diagnostic option provides customers with a thorough understanding of the issue with their vehicle and the peace of mind that comes with knowing their car is in good hands.

Repair service

The Repair Service is an ideal option for customers who need to repair their vehicle. To schedule a Repair Service appointment, the customer enters their quote number (if provided) and the system confirms whether the quote exists or not. If the quote exists, the customer can then schedule a service appointment during the available time slot.The Repair Service option ensures that customers receive the necessary repairs to their vehicle in a timely and efficient manner.

1000km Service

During the 1000 km Service, the vehicle will be thoroughly inspected and serviced to ensure that all components are in good condition. The 1000 km Service is a valuable option for customers who want to maintain their vehicle's reliability and longevity.
